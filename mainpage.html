Yvonne, Raven, and Chloe have always shared a deep passion for fashion and storytelling through design. The idea to create wedding dresses sparked during a trip to Paris, where they were inspired by the elegance of vintage bridal gowns in a small atelier. That experience ignited their dream to design dresses that celebrate love, individuality, and timeless beauty. They began sketching ideas on napkins in cafés and collecting fabrics from local markets, envisioning a bridal collection that would blend tradition with modern artistry.
Their journey was anything but easy. After years of working in fashion design separately, they reunited to pursue their shared dream. From late nights perfecting silhouettes to sourcing sustainable materials, every step brought them closer to opening their own boutique. They took a leap of faith, pooled their savings, and launched their wedding dress shop in a charming heritage shophouse—transforming it into a space where brides could feel both inspired and cherished.
Their wedding dresses are known for their delicate craftsmanship, intricate lacework, and flattering, versatile cuts that cater to modern brides with classic tastes. The shop itself is an elegant, serene haven filled with soft natural light, curated floral arrangements, and personal touches that reflect their dedication to creating a memorable experience. Each dress tells a story—just like Yvonne, Raven, and Chloe’s own journey from dreamers to designers.
